My Role

To expand knowledge in the UX design field and deepen my graphic skills, I took the course “UI for UX Designers” by Careerfoundry. During the course I went through the whole process of designing the user interface of a responsive web app from scratch. And the outcome is the Perfect Properties.

Perfect Properties

Perfect Properties is a responsive web app that provides property buyers with information on properties of interest.

User Flows & Low Fidelity Wireframes: Mobile First

With the project brief at hand, I began with informal competitive analysis of other real estate apps and created the most important user flows for my persona, which then led me to the low fidelity wireframes and prototype for mobile breakpoint.

High Fidelity Wireframes

Moodboard

The persona of the project Rashida is looking for financial security for her family. As she is the first time investor, she needs something clear and simple. Rashida is making a good living, so she‘s looking not simply for a house, but she wants to expand wealth of her family. White color symbolizes safety. Blue – reliability and security. Green stimulates harmony and helps to make decisions. So the color palette should reflect Rashida’s state of mind and feelings. The objects on the moodboard represent simplicity and luxury at the same time. The imagery on the board says that everything is managable and easy even for the novice as Rashida.
