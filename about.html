My Story

During a long year experience in Software Testing, I have developed strong analytical skills and an eye for details. All the usability bugs at the late stage of development and the empathy for the end user have turned my way into UX design. I believe that a good design (which makes the product usable, useful, intuitive and aesthetic) adds a great value to people’s everyday life.

Despite my passion for making users happy, I love being outdoors and have long discovery walks with my dog. I’m always open to new experiences and enjoy travels.
